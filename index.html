Hack Camp

Apply Now

A two week experience with multiple start dates between June 1st - August 10th

We'll bring you from your 1st line of code to publishing your first web application on the internet.

When / how long is Hack Camp?

Hack Camp is 2 weeks long. There are multiple start dates between June 1st and August 10th.

For example, some start dates include June 1st, June 15th, June 29th, etc.

Where is Hack Camp?

The camp is in San Francisco. Upon acceptance we will send you the specific location details.

How much does it cost?

We're currently finalizing the cost of the program and we will reach out to all of our applicants with more information as soon as possible.

Do I need to be in high school?

Yes! Any current high school student who wants learn. Current meaning including those who will be high school 1st years or college 1st years in the fall. We may be able to make exceptions if you (contact us).

What if I don't know how to code?

Perfect! This is the perfect opportunity to learn! We'll have experienced mentors available at all hours and are targetting the program towards people with little to no experience.

FAQ

What do I need to bring?

You should bring your laptop, lunch, and a thirst for learning. Laptops will be provided by request if you do not have access to one (contact us).

What if I already know how to code?

There's always more to learn—why not learn more surrounded by awesome makers and doers with similar interests?

What types of activities will students do outside of coding?

Students will participate in team bonding activities, tech company tours, hands-on hacking, and more!
